About

What: WiCS Hacks is an all female, 12-hour Hackathon

When: 10am - 10pm Feburary 17, 2018

Where: 6th floor of GDC

Some things to note:

  • We welcome all female-identifying students
  • Priority will be given to computer science majors. However, feel free to register if you are another major - we will notify all applicants by email once they are accepted.
  • NO previous hackathon experience is necessary. Every experience level is encouraged to attend, including beginners and those with more hackathon experience.
  • Teams must be between 1-4 people. You do NOT have to have a team formed to attend. If you do not have a team but would like one, team formation will occur at the start of the event.
